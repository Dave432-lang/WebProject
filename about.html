About Tech Connect 2025

Tech Connect 2025 is Pentecost University's flagship innovation summit, bringing together technology enthusiasts, industry professionals, and aspiring innovators for two days of immersive learning, networking, and collaboration.

🎯

Our Vision

To create Ghana's premier technology and innovation platform that bridges the gap between academia and industry, fostering a culture of technological excellence and entrepreneurship among students and professionals.

💡

Our Mission

To empower the next generation of tech leaders by providing access to cutting-edge knowledge, mentorship from industry experts, and opportunities to showcase innovative solutions to real-world challenges.

2025 Theme: "Innovation Without Boundaries"

This year's theme celebrates the limitless potential of technology to transform lives, break barriers, and create sustainable solutions for Africa's most pressing challenges. We explore how emerging technologies like AI, blockchain, IoT, and cloud computing are reshaping industries and opening new possibilities.

About Pentech Hub

Pentech Hub is Pentecost University's dedicated innovation and technology center, established to nurture the next generation of African tech leaders and entrepreneurs. As the primary organizer of Tech Connect 2025, Pentech Hub brings together its expertise in tech education, industry partnerships, and community building.

The hub serves as a collaborative space where students, faculty, and industry professionals come together to learn, innovate, and create solutions that address local and global challenges. Through various programs, workshops, and events like Tech Connect, Pentech Hub is committed to building a thriving tech ecosystem in Ghana and beyond.

Key Focus Areas: Software Development, Data Science, Cybersecurity, AI & Machine Learning, Digital Entrepreneurship, and Innovation Management.
